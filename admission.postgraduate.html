WELCOME TO HIGHER INSTITUTION PROJECT POSTGRADUATE ONLINE APPLICATION
Please be informed that the form is still available for those who want to resume in January 2019

Dear Prospective Student,
Admission into Covenant University is STRICTLY by merit. We don't ask for money to influence admission. Please beware.

Before you apply, check the Programs webpage for information on academic programs and application deadlines. For additional information, please go to the application procedure page.

APPLY FOR ADMISSION
Create an account

Click Here to Create an Account.

if you already have an Account, click Here to login your credentials that will enable you to complete your application over several sessions. You will access your secure application in subsequent sessions by signing in under "User Log in." If you have started an application previously, you can always login to continue. The system will prompt you on how to complete the application. Once satisfied with the application, click on submit!

UPLOAD OF REQUIRED APPLICATION DOCUMENTS

The online upload of required application documents is necessary, it is therefore advised that you please come along with the originals of this uploaded documents for sighting during your interview. Your application information is maintained on a secure server and remains confidential prior to submission. Use the Support link to get help or assistance during the completion and submission process.

REVIEW OF THE APPLICATION

Your application will be reviewed online by the appropriate admission officials once submitted to Covenant University Admission Office Online. All required application procedures must be completed on or before the appropriate application deadline.

THE STATUS OF YOUR APPLICATION

Check the status of your application by logging into this system with the login details assigned to you for the application. However, you will receive important email messages as it relates to your application status.

QUESTIONS

Use the link provided above to get more information on academic program offerings, as well as application instructions and deadlines.
