GREAT REASONS TO JOIN US
Enjoy Motivation Working Conditions

At Higher Institution Project, we believe that collective success is an integral part of the wellbeing of all in the workplace. Whatever the environment in which our employees work, we strive to offer them stimulating working conditions so they can give the best of themselves: safe and pleasant spaces, motivating remuneration, social protection, respect for work/life balance.

Enrich Your Skills and Develop Your Potentials

Higher Institution Project invest in the potential of all our employees because they are the true drivers of our growth and carry our ambitions in the instituion and every part of the world. We offer them a broad array of training courses to develop their skills throughout their career, enabling them to achieve a high level of operational performance and broaden their professional horizons. This proactive approach, at the heart of the pioneering spirit driving the instituion since its creation in 2019, explains why it is today among the top 50 most innovative instituion in Africa.

Contribute to the Country's Economic Development

Higher Institution Project has set itself an ambitious goal: ....., to supply reliable, affordable goods and services across the country and the globe.
